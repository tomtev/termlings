# Goal

Run an autonomous company operating system that converts strategy into measurable outcomes.

## Near-Term Focus
- Keep executive priorities explicit and ranked.
- Improve execution speed without creating coordination debt.
- Build a durable operating cadence across product, engineering, growth, and finance.
- Keep owner interruptions minimal and high-signal.

## Working Principles
- One clear owner per initiative.
- Weekly planning, daily execution, visible accountability.
- Escalate internally first; contact `human:default` only for env keys/credentials or key owner decisions.
